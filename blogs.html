The Art of Just Starting

May 6, 2026

| "You don't have to have it all figured out. Just start"

We come across this quote quite often, while listening to motivational speakers or while scrolling through Instagram, but we really don't take it seriously. It's quite obvious—those two words “Just Start” might appear vague to many, like what do you even mean? How can I start? I don't even know the ABCs of this thing. But really, you don't need to have it all figured out, trust me. This quote is meant for people who struggle with taking action—it's quite normal nowadays, where people are more bothered about perfectionism, judgement, wanting to know it all, but reality is the opposite.
